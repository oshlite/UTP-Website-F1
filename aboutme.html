Sarah Mitchell

Formula 1 Journalist & Digital Content Creator

My Journey

With over a decade of experience covering Formula 1, I've had the privilege of witnessing some of the most iconic moments in motorsport history. My passion for F1 began during the early 2000s, and since then, I've dedicated my career to bringing the excitement and technical brilliance of this sport to fans worldwide.

As a journalist and content creator, I specialize in providing in-depth analysis, behind-the-scenes stories, and exclusive interviews with drivers and team principals. My work has been featured in leading motorsport publications, and I've covered over 150 Grand Prix weekends across the globe.

Through this website, I aim to share my knowledge and insights while creating a community for F1 enthusiasts to engage, learn, and celebrate the sport we all love.
